Instructions

Click "Join Red Team" or "Join Blue Team" to pick a side. If someone is already on that team, they will become a spectator.

Once you've joined a team, click and drag to move your mallet.

As physics are only processed locally for your mallet and goal, you won't be able to interact with/score on the other team until someone joins that team.
